About SVS

SVS is a company known for high-performance subwoofers and speakers that bring home-theater level bass into regular rooms and living spaces. Their lineup includes compact sealed subs, powerful ported subs, and models designed for both music and movies.

This website focuses on a few popular sealed SVS models and uses their approximate enclosure dimensions to help you plan room layout and available free space along your walls.

You can learn more about SVS products on the official site: svsound.com .
