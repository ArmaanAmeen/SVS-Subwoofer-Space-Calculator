Subwoofer Setup Tips

Good placement and setup can make a huge difference in bass quality. These tips are general guidelines you can use along with the calculator.

  1. Start by placing the subwoofer along the same wall as your front speakers.
  2. Avoid pushing the cabinet directly into the corner; leave a little breathing room.
  3. Try the “subwoofer crawl” method to find smoother bass positions in the room.
  4. Keep the sub out of doorways or tight alcoves that can rattle or buzz.
  5. Use your AVR or a measurement app to set crossover and level properly.
  6. Experiment with phase and polarity settings if your AVR provides them.
  7. Once you find a good spot, re-measure your free wall space using this site.
  8. Consider isolation feet or a platform if you live in an apartment or upstairs.
